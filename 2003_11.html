HPUX平台上软件按装

Install：

swinstall -s $(pwd)/software-verinfo.hpux.depot software

Register the depot:

swreg -l depot $(pwd)/software-verinfo.hpux.depot


Copy the product to an existing depot:

swcopy -s $(pwd)/software-verinfo.hpux.depot software @ /your/depot
